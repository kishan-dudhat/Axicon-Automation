ABOUT US

Axicon Automation Industries Limited

Axicon Automation delivers advanced laser and industrial automation solutions, including Fiber Laser Marking, Cutting, Welding, CO₂ Engraving Machines, and customized systems. Our solutions are widely used across automotive, semiconductor, packaging, plastics, and medical industries, ensuring precision, speed, and reliability. We design smart systems like rotary marking, HMI-controlled setups, conveyors, and On-the-Fly (OTF) technology for smooth, high-speed production.

With a focus on quality and innovation, we use modern technology and high-grade materials to build reliable machines. Easy to install and operate, our solutions reduce manual work, improve efficiency, and ensure consistent performance. Axicon Automation is committed to delivering dependable systems that support long-term industrial growth.
